How to Play the Language Test

1. Game Rounds

The game consists of 5 rounds with decreasing time limits: 20s, 18s, 16s, 14s, and 12s.

2. Target Letter

Write words starting with the target letter shown on the screen.

3. Submit Words

Type a word and press the SPACEBAR to submit it.

4. Scoring Rules

Words must be unique and cannot have repeated characters (e.g., “ddd”). Repeated or invalid words will not increase your score.

5. Objective

Try to score as many points as possible by entering valid words in all rounds!
