How to Play the Attention Test

1. Game Duration

The test runs for 10 seconds per round, during which multiple objects will move across the screen.

2. Target Objects

Focus on counting specific target objects like buses, cars, bikes, and cycles that appear among distractions.

3. Movement

Objects move in various directions and speeds, making it important to pay close attention.

4. Input

After the timer ends, enter the number of each target object you counted to the best of your ability.

5. Objective

Try to count accurately and improve your attention and focus over time!
