How to Play the Perception Test

🔍 Objective

Your goal is to quickly find and click on the target object (e.g., a red circle) hidden among distractors.

👁️ What Will Happen

Each round shows a grid full of shapes. One of them will match the required target — the others are distractors.

⚡ What You Need to Do

Click on the correct target object as fast as possible before time runs out.

⏱️ Time Limit

You have limited time per round. Finding the target quickly helps you get a perfect score!

🏆 Scoring

+1 point for a correct guess, -1 for a wrong one, and +2 bonus if you finish the round perfectly before time ends.
