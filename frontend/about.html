About Mental Cure

Mental Cure is an innovative online platform dedicated to helping users monitor and improve their mental wellbeing. By offering a collection of carefully designed interactive games, Mental Cure allows you to assess your stress levels in a fun and engaging way. Beyond assessment, the platform provides personalized activities aimed at reducing stress, improving focus, and enhancing your overall mental health.

Our games are based on psychological research and cognitive science, tailored to provide insights into your stress patterns while encouraging healthy mental habits.

Key Features

  • Wide variety of cognitive and perception games targeting stress and focus.
  • Personalized stress level analysis based on gameplay data.
  • Simple, clean, and modern interface for an immersive experience.
  • Progress tracking over time to monitor improvements.
  • Helpful tips and exercises for stress management after assessments.
  • Accessible on all devices with responsive design.
